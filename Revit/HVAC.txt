This Revit MEP model was created to coordinate HVAC systems within the building structure. The project includes ductwork, equipment placement, and space management. The model helps identify clashes and improves installation accuracy during construction.

HVAC LAYOUT

HVAC Duct Layout Design – Project Description
Complete Air Distribution System
This drawing illustrates a complete HVAC ductwork layout for a building floor. The system distributes conditioned air from Fan Coil Units (FCUs) to multiple rooms through a network of supply and return air ducts.
Efficient Duct Routing
The duct routes have been designed to provide balanced airflow while minimizing pressure losses. The layout follows the building architecture and avoids conflicts with walls, corridors, and occupied spaces.
Air Terminal Placement
Supply air diffusers and return air grilles are strategically positioned to ensure uniform air distribution, thermal comfort, and proper air circulation throughout the occupied areas.
HVAC Equipment Integration
The drawing includes FCUs, duct accessories, and airflow control components required for system operation. These elements are coordinated to maintain efficient cooling and ventilation performance.
