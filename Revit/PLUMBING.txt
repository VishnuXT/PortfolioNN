PLUMBING 3D 

Water Supply Distribution
= The plumbing layout distributes clean water from the main supply line to all fixtures such as wash basins, sinks, toilets, and other sanitary equipment throughout the building.

Drainage and Wastewater Collection

= Drainage pipes collect wastewater from bathrooms, kitchens, and other plumbing fixtures and transport it to the main sewer connection for disposal.

Vertical Risers and Branch Connections

=The vertical pipes (risers) connect different floors, while branch pipes extend from the risers to individual fixtures, ensuring water reaches every room efficiently.
